# Code of Conduct

We expect contributors and maintainers to keep project discussions respectful, direct, and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be considerate when giving and receiving feedback.
- Assume good intent, but accept responsibility for impact.
- Keep technical disagreement specific and evidence-based.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruption of project discussions.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns through the security or maintainer contact channel listed in `SECURITY.md`.
